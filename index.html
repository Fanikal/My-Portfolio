My introduction

As a Human-Computer Interaction and Design student with a background in Computer Science, I'm driven to unite business, design and technology. My journey has taught me how to collaborate with multidisciplinary teams, offering a unique perspective to understand and appreciate diverse viewpoints, keeping empathy as my core value.
